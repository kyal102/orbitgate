# Safety and Flight-Use Boundary

OrbitGate Lite is a software demonstration for deterministic verification of AI-generated orbital and satellite claims. It is not certified, validated, approved, or intended for operational aerospace use.

## Do Not Use For

- Spacecraft command and control.
- Launch, deorbit, maneuver, collision-avoidance, or mission-readiness decisions.
- Replacing STK, GMAT, Orekit, certified flight dynamics tools, mission-control systems, or qualified engineering review.
- Defence, intelligence, regulatory, or safety-critical workflows without independent certification.

## Intended Use

- Local evaluation of the OrbitGate interface.
- Developer testing of route, iframe, and proxy integration.
- Research demos of deterministic verification gates.
- Education around why AI-generated aerospace claims need boundaries and replayable evidence.

## Interpretation

Treat every output as a demonstration artifact. A pass, block, warning, benchmark result, certificate, or evidence pack in this repo does not prove flight readiness or real-world orbital safety.
